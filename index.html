FAQ?

What is CryptoStop, and how does it work?

CryptoStop is an advanced ransomware detection tool that safeguards your digital world from ransomware attacks. It works by analyzing the behavior of software running on your system. It monitors entropy changes, CPU and power consumption fluctuations, and background behavior to detect and prevent ransomware in real-time. Unlike traditional antivirus software that relies on known malware signatures, CryptoStop focuses on identifying ransomware based on its actions and behavior.

How can I install CryptoStop on my computer or network?

Installing CryptoStop is a straightforward process. Visit our Downloads page and follow the step-by-step installation instructions provided for your specific operating system. We also offer installation guides and documentation to assist you throughout the process.

What sets CryptoStop apart from traditional antivirus software?

CryptoStop distinguishes itself from traditional antivirus software through its behavior-based detection approach. Unlike antivirus programs that rely on known malware signatures, CryptoStop identifies ransomware based on how it behaves. This means CryptoStop can detect both known and emerging ransomware threats. Our tool is designed to minimize false positives, ensuring that you are alerted to genuine threats without unnecessary disruptions to your workflow.

Can CryptoStop coexist with other antivirus or cybersecurity software on my system?

Yes, CryptoStop is designed to work alongside other antivirus and cybersecurity software. It complements your existing security infrastructure by providing behavior-based ransomware detection. You can confidently run CryptoStop alongside your preferred antivirus solutions, enhancing your overall protection against a wide range of threats without conflicts.
